Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 26, 2024

Last Updated: December 26, 2024

Welcome to Hasio ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Hasio (the "App"), your guide to Al-Ahsa Oasis, Saudi Arabia.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password.
  • Profile Information: Optional profile details such as your preferred language (English/Arabic).
  • User Content: Photos, notes, and travel moments you choose to save in the app.
  • Voice Data: When using the voice assistant feature, your voice input is processed to provide travel recommendations.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Features used, pages visited, and interaction patterns within the app.
  • Location Data: Only when you explicitly add location to your travel moments (optional feature).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's features and functionality
  • To process your voice queries and provide travel recommendations
  • To save your favorite places, travel moments, and trip plans
  • To authenticate your account and provide personalized experiences
  • To communicate with you about app updates and features
  • To improve our services and user experience

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

  • Supabase: For user authentication and secure data storage
  • ElevenLabs: For text-to-speech voice responses
  • Groq: For speech-to-text transcription and AI-powered responses
  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In: For optional social authentication

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

4. Voice Data Processing

When you use the voice assistant:

  • Your voice is recorded only when you actively press the microphone button
  • Voice recordings are processed in real-time and not permanently stored
  • Transcribed text may be temporarily stored to maintain conversation context
  • Voice data is processed by our third-party speech services (Groq, ElevenLabs)

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely using Supabase with encryption at rest and in transit
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • User-uploaded photos are stored in secure cloud storage
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • You can delete your account and associated data at any time through the app settings
  • Voice recordings are processed in real-time and not retained after processing

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of optional features like voice assistant

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
